# PRODUCT.md — skalez.ai

## Product
**Name:** skalez.ai  
**Tagline:** AI news, terminal-native.  
**Category:** AI news curation / daily drops  
**Platform:** Instagram (primary), daily carousels  

## Users
Developers, builders, AI enthusiasts, tech-curious students. People who want AI news without the corporate fluff. They appreciate precision, speed, and minimalism. They live in terminals, IDEs, and dark-mode UIs.

## Product Purpose
Deliver the most important AI news of the day in a format that feels native to the tools developers already use. No hype language. No gradients. No corporate speak. Just raw signal.

## Tone & Voice
- **Precise:** Every word earns its place.
- **Hype (but earned):** Bold claims backed by numbers. "72.5% on SWE-bench."
- **Terminal-native:** `$` prompts, `>` commands, `[TAGS]`, status codes.
- **Anti-corporate:** No "we're excited to announce." No emojis. No exclamation points.

## Anti-References
- Gradient overlays on images
- Glassmorphism / blur effects
- Purple-blue AI gradients
- SaaS cream-and-orange template
- Identical card grids
- Hero-metric template (big number, small label)

## Strategic Principles
1. **Terminal is the aesthetic.** The post should look like a screenshot from a macOS Terminal window — simplified, like Claude's interface.
2. **B&W only for chrome.** All UI chrome (text, borders, background) is pure black and white. Only media assets (images) are in color.
3. **ANSI Shadow headers.** Big bold ASCII block text for brand/section headers. Clean monospaced body.
4. **Maximal minimalism.** Strip everything that doesn't communicate. No frames, no decorations, no chrome that doesn't serve hierarchy.
5. **FOMO as engine.** Every post creates urgency. "Save this. You'll need it tomorrow."

## Register
**Brand.** Design IS the product. The visual identity is what followers recognize and share.
